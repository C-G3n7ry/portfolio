About

          
        


        

          

            

              I'm Christopher Gentry — most places online I go by
              g3n7ry.
            

            

              I live in Rapid City, South Dakota, at the edge of
              the Black Hills, and I spend my time on two things that turn out to
              be the same thing: researching local history until it's clear,
              and building software until it's simple. Both reward patience and
              punish hand-waving.
            

            

              The writing lives at
              christopher-gentry.com —
              a growing archive of the people, places, and decisions that built the
              Gateway to the Black Hills. The building shows up here, and on
              GitHub.
